On improving your skills.

Here it is plain and simple. If you want to improve your skills you need to Read , Write and Practice.

Reading

You should be aiming to read between 2-3 hours a day outside of school or your normal job. Look for blogs , articles , technical books any source of material that you will enjoy. I like to split my reading sessions up. I will usually try to read something simple or easy. Material that I have already mastered. It serves to keep your skills up to date and to reinforce the stuff that you have already learn't. After I read the "easier stuff" I will look for more challenging material. This is stuff that is new to me and that I am not familiar with. I will read until I am mentally exhausted and then I will take a break. After this l rinse and repeat another day. The idea here is that the difficult material I studied the previous day is now more familiar to me and by reading it again I am reinforcing the learning that has already taken place all the while pushing forward with new challenging material. It is important to keep challenging yourself with suitably difficult material.

Write

Write a blog or a journal or anything. Take a piece of paper with you when you study and write out challenging stuff. By doing this you are reinforcing your learning. Even better write a technical blog. In researching and preparing your material you are yet again learning new technologies and reinforcing the stuff you already know.

Practice

Last but not least. You must practice.This is the only way you will get better.
